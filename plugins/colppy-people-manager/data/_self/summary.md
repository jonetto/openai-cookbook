## My Leadership Profile

**From this session:** I led a high-stakes coordination meeting across 8 managers covering multiple business crises simultaneously (NPS, MRR, cross-sell, DB migration, people departures). I drive accountability by assigning public ownership and naming standards clearly — I raised the action item discipline question directly and assigned Alejandro as the weekly progress presenter.

**What I don't yet know about myself in this role:** Whether my directness in accountability conversations lands as empowering or pressuring for each person. My instinct is to tighten structure when results are off; the question is whether that's calibrated to the person.

## My Execution Patterns

- I created clear accountability structures in the meeting: weekly presenter role, specific MRR baselines, Friday deadline for 50% test completion. High-structure approach under pressure.
- I covered significant agenda ground in one session — good at holding the thread across topics.
- I moved fast. May not have given each topic — or each person — adequate space.

## Active Development Themes

1. **Delegation and trust** — Stop resolving things myself when performance disappoints. The bottleneck is me.
