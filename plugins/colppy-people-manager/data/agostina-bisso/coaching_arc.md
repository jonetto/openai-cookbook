## Regression patterns

_Too early to identify stable regression patterns. Initial signals to watch:_

- **Personal boundaries vs. organizational standards:** The Thursday presencialidad refusal — coming very early in her tenure, before building political capital — may be a one-time friction or a pattern of prioritizing personal constraints over team norms. The risk: if she establishes early that rules don't fully apply to her, it erodes her authority to hold standards for others.
- **Plan completeness:** The Customer Plan 2026 was delivered iteratively but with significant gaps (no North Star with targets, no capacity model, no activation definitions by ICP). This could be a pacing issue (reasonable given how new she is) or a depth issue (she frames well but doesn't fully spec). Need more data points to distinguish.
- **Visibility of execution:** She builds plans and frameworks, but the connection between plan → weekly execution → measured progress isn't yet visible. The scorecard doesn't exist yet. This may simply be sequencing (diagnostic before operation) but needs to resolve quickly.
- **Solo solving tendency (new — Feb 23 feedforward):** Juan flagged that she has "mucha tendencia a resolver vos" when she sees gaps in other areas. Risk of becoming a silo rather than elevating peers. She acknowledged it but hasn't demonstrated the behavioral change yet. This connects to the cross-functional collaboration gap in SUMA (En Desarrollo in Multiplica el Impacto).
