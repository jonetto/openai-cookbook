### Block B — Change management and culture / mindset

   - "We agreed: more brain power on planning, weekly iterations, data and context in every update. The migration is the test. **Transition:**

- "That’s why I’ve been going deep on the migration. I’ve been looking at the same platform, the same kind of complexity you’re describing, to test whether the plan holds up. I want to walk through that with you so we’re aligned on what I’m seeing."
