The growth needed: connecting product decisions to commercial outcomes — MRR, activation, NPS recovery, sales velocity. 4. **Cultural integration: ownership vs. process** — He comes from structured environments (La Nación, 8 years of guardias). Colppy's culture expects organic ownership without formal incentives. He's adapting his behavior but may not yet have internalized the belief. Watch for whether he truly shifts or just complies.

5. **Talent and team development** — The widest gap in the matrix (SUMA Standard self-rating vs. En Desarrollo from Juan, with 8/9 behaviors FALSE). He doesn't yet give structured feedback, create development plans, or use coaching as a leadership tool. This is a critical growth area as his team scales.
