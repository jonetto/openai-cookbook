- **Reports the "what" but not the "so what."** Brings data points (92% activation, 23/36 products, 5% validation) without trend analysis, root-cause interpretation, or corrective proposals. Juan has coached this repeatedly since November.
- **Emotionally invested.** Genuinely cares about outcomes. Expresses frustration when targets aren't met ("feeling stuck despite defending the product since onboarding"). This energy is an asset but channels into emotion rather than structured problem-solving.
- **Collaborative but invisible.** Generates real value in 1:1s with Franco, Agus, and Anto, but this work doesn't surface in group meetings. Juan noted she "isn't showing her work" even though she pushes initiatives across all fronts.
- **Reactive to structure, not proactive about it.** Accepts frameworks when Juan provides them (JTBD, palancas → iniciativas → KRs) but doesn't create her own. Her weekly updates are task lists, not strategic dashboards.
- **Follow-through gaps.** Multiple action items persist across meetings with missed deadlines: plan de sueldos (Jan 9 → still open Mar 10), ebook (Nov → disappeared), 31 user calls (Dec → no follow-up), roadmap with Ale Soto (Jan 9 → unclear).
- **Receptive to coaching.** Never defensive. Accepts feedback gracefully. But absorption-to-behavior conversion is low — the same gaps identified in November are still present in March.
- **Distance from office.** Does not work from BA office daily. This creates additional friction for visibility and collaboration, especially in a culture increasingly valuing presencialidad.
